Ferruccio Lamborghini

The man and his dreams (1916 – 1993)

Ferruccio Lamborghini (28 April 1916 – 20 February 1993) was an Italian automobile designer, inventor, mechanic, engineer, winemaker, industrialist and businessman.

Some of Ferruccio Lamborghini accomplishments :

1943 to 1946 World War II

Ferruccio was drafted into the Italian Air Force where he was stationed on the Island of Rhodes. During the war he was captured by the British army. They put him to work as a mechanic. After the war he was released and sent home saftely from the British.When he got home he started a small repair shop.

1948 Lamborghini Trattori

Ferruccio made enough money from his small tractor repair business that he was able to start mass producing his own tractors in his factory.

1963 Automobili Lamborghini

Ferruccio Lamborghini Founded in 1963 to compete with Ferrari. The company was noted for using a rear mid-engine, rear-wheel drive layout. Lamborghini grew rapidly during its first decade, but sales plunged in the wake of the 1973 worldwide financial downturn and the oil crisis.

1974 The Lamborghini Countach

The Lamborghini Countach is a rear mid-engine, rear-wheel-drive sports car produced by the Italian automobile manufacturer Lamborghini from 1974 until 1990. It is one of the many exotic designs developed by Italian design house Bertone, which pioneered and popularized the sharply angled "Italian Wedge" shape.
